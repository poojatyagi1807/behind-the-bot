E-COMMERCE PLATFORM RETURNS, REFUNDS AND EXCHANGE POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective March 2024

SECTION 1: RETURN WINDOW AND ELIGIBILITY

1.1 STANDARD RETURN WINDOW
Most items may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund to the original payment method. The item must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags attached.

1.2 EXTENDED RETURN WINDOW
Premium members receive a 60-day return window on all eligible items. Items must still be in original, unused condition.

1.3 NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
The following categories are not eligible for return:
- Perishable goods including food, flowers, and plants
- Digital downloads and software licenses once accessed
- Personal hygiene items including underwear, swimwear, and earrings (for health reasons)
- Hazardous materials including certain cleaning supplies and batteries
- Items marked as "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Custom or personalized items made to order

1.4 DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
Items that arrive damaged or defective may be returned outside the standard window. Customers must report damage within 7 days of delivery with photographic evidence. A full refund or replacement will be offered at no cost to the customer, including return shipping.

SECTION 2: RETURN PROCESS

2.1 Customers must initiate a return via the online portal or customer support chat. Returns cannot be processed in-store.

2.2 Upon approval, the customer will receive a prepaid return shipping label via email within 2 business hours.

2.3 Items must be shipped within 7 days of receiving the return label. Labels expire after 7 days.

2.4 Customers are responsible for packaging the item securely. Items damaged in return transit due to inadequate packaging may not receive a full refund.

2.5 Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. A $6.99 return shipping fee applies to preference-based returns (changed mind, wrong size ordered by customer).

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SECTION 3: REFUND PROCESSING

3.1 Refunds are initiated within 2 business days of receiving the returned item at our warehouse.

3.2 Refunds are returned to the original payment method: credit/debit card (3-5 business days), PayPal (1-2 business days), store credit (immediate).

3.3 Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item sent, defective item).

3.4 Gift returns are refunded as store credit to the gift recipient, not to the original purchaser.

3.5 Partial refunds may be issued for items returned in less than original condition, at platform discretion.

SECTION 4: EXCHANGES

4.1 Exchanges for a different size or color of the same item are processed free of charge within the standard return window.

4.2 Exchanges for a different item require a return and new purchase.

4.3 If the exchange item costs more than the original, the customer pays the difference. If less, the difference is refunded.

4.4 Exchange items ship within 1-2 business days of receiving the original return.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SECTION 5: ORDER CANCELLATIONS

5.1 Orders may be cancelled within 1 hour of placement for a full refund with no questions asked.

5.2 After 1 hour, orders that have not yet shipped may be cancelled by contacting support. Refund is processed within 24 hours.

5.3 Orders that have already shipped cannot be cancelled. The customer must wait for delivery and initiate a return.

5.4 Pre-order cancellations are accepted at any time before the item ships.

SECTION 6: SELLER-FULFILLED ORDERS

6.1 Items sold by third-party sellers have their own return policies, which must meet minimum platform standards (minimum 14-day return window).

6.2 Platform guarantee covers all purchases: if a seller does not honor their policy, the platform will step in to resolve the issue.

6.3 Customers should initiate returns for seller-fulfilled items through the same platform portal.

SECTION 7: FRAUD PREVENTION

7.1 Accounts with more than 5 returns in a 90-day period will be reviewed. Excessive returns may result in account restrictions.

7.2 Customers attempting to return items not purchased on the platform will have their accounts suspended.

7.3 Return fraud, including returning used items as new or switching products, may result in account termination and legal action.
