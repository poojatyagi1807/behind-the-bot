SECTION 1: CANCELLATION POLICY TIERS

Hosts on this platform choose one of five cancellation policies at the time of listing. The guest's refund eligibility depends on which policy was active when the booking was made.

1.1 FLEXIBLE POLICY
Guests receive a full refund of the nightly rate if they cancel at least 24 hours before the check-in time (shown in the listing's timezone). Cancellations made within 24 hours of check-in are non-refundable. The cleaning fee is always refunded if the guest cancels before check-in. The service fee is refunded on the first cancellation only.

1.2 MODERATE POLICY
Guests receive a full refund if they cancel at least 5 days before check-in. If the guest cancels between 1 and 5 days before check-in, the first night is non-refundable and 50% of the remaining nights are refunded. Cancellations on the day of check-in or after are fully non-refundable. Cleaning fees are refunded if cancelled before check-in.

1.3 STRICT POLICY
Guests receive a 50% refund (minus the service fee) if they cancel at least 14 days before check-in. If cancelled between 7 and 14 days before check-in, the guest receives a 25% refund of the nightly rate. Cancellations made within 7 days of check-in receive no refund. The service fee is non-refundable.

1.4 LONG-TERM STRICT POLICY
Applies to bookings of 28 nights or more. Guests receive a full refund if cancelled within 48 hours of booking AND at least 28 days before check-in. Otherwise, the first 30 nights are non-refundable. Remaining nights are refunded.

1.5 NON-REFUNDABLE POLICY
Guests who select the non-refundable option receive a discounted rate (typically 10% lower). These bookings are not eligible for a refund under any standard circumstances. Extenuating circumstances may still apply (see Section 3).

SECTION 2: SERVICE FEES

2.1 The platform service fee is typically 14-16% of the booking subtotal, charged to the guest.

2.2 Service fees are refunded if the guest cancels before check-in under a Flexible or Moderate policy with adequate notice.

2.3 Service fees are non-refundable for Strict and Long-Term Strict policies, regardless of when the guest cancels.

2.4 In the event of a platform-initiated cancellation (technical error, host cancellation), the service fee is always fully refunded.

SECTION 3: EXTENUATING CIRCUMSTANCES POLICY

Extenuating circumstances allow guests or hosts to cancel without penalty when unexpected events beyond their control prevent a stay.

3.1 QUALIFYING CIRCUMSTANCES
The following events may qualify as extenuating circumstances:
- Unexpected serious illness or injury of the guest or an immediate family member, requiring hospitalization
- Death of a host, guest, or immediate family member
- Jury duty or court subpoena that cannot be postponed
- Government-mandated travel restrictions or border closures
- Natural disasters including earthquakes, hurricanes, wildfires, or floods that directly affect the listing or prevent travel
- Mandatory evacuations ordered by government authorities
- Declared states of emergency that affect the destination

3.2 NON-QUALIFYING CIRCUMSTANCES
The following events do NOT qualify as extenuating circumstances:
- Bad weather that does not result in a government travel advisory
- Changing one's mind or personal preference changes
- Work commitments or schedule changes
- Transportation delays not caused by government restriction
- Undisclosed pre-existing medical conditions
- Visa or passport issues due to personal circumstances

3.3 DOCUMENTATION REQUIRED
Guests claiming extenuating circumstances must submit supporting documentation within 14 days of cancellation. Accepted documents include: medical certificates from licensed physicians, death certificates, court summons, government travel advisories, or evacuation orders. The platform reserves the right to request additional documentation.

3.4 REVIEW PROCESS
Extenuating circumstance claims are reviewed within 5-7 business days. Approved claims result in a full refund of the nightly rate and cleaning fee. The service fee refund is at platform discretion.

SECTION 4: HOST CANCELLATIONS

4.1 If a host cancels a confirmed booking, the guest receives a full refund including service fees.

4.2 Hosts who cancel within 30 days of check-in are subject to penalties: a $100 flat fee for cancellations 8-30 days out, and a $300 fee for cancellations 7 days or less before check-in.

4.3 The platform will assist affected guests in finding alternative accommodation when a host cancels within 72 hours of check-in.

4.4 Hosts who frequently cancel may have their listings suspended or removed.

SECTION 5: LISTING INACCURACIES AND MISREPRESENTATION

5.1 Guests who arrive and find that a listing significantly differs from its description may be eligible for a full or partial refund.

5.2 Significant inaccuracies include: incorrect number of bedrooms or bathrooms, missing advertised amenities (pool, hot tub, parking), different location than listed, safety hazards not disclosed, or presence of pets not mentioned in listing.

5.3 Guests must report inaccuracies within 24 hours of check-in. Claims submitted after 24 hours are not eligible.

5.4 The platform will contact the host to verify the claim. If confirmed, the guest may be offered a full refund or rebooking assistance.

5.5 Minor inaccuracies (e.g., slightly different décor, minor amenity differences) are not eligible for refunds.

SECTION 6: PARTIAL STAYS AND EARLY DEPARTURES

6.1 If a guest checks in but departs early, the refund is governed by the host's cancellation policy applied to the unused nights.

6.2 Under a Flexible policy, unused nights after a 24-hour departure notice are refunded.

6.3 Under a Strict policy, early departure is non-refundable regardless of notice.

6.4 Guests who leave early due to a listing inaccuracy or safety issue should contact support within 24 hours of departure to initiate a claim.

SECTION 7: REFUND PROCESSING

7.1 Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

7.2 The platform does not issue refunds to a different payment method than the one used for booking.

7.3 Refund amounts are calculated based on the exchange rate at the time of the original booking for international transactions.

7.4 Guests will receive an email confirmation when a refund is initiated. Processing times depend on the guest's bank or card issuer.

7.5 Refunds above $5,000 may require additional identity verification.

SECTION 8: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 If a guest and host cannot agree on a refund, either party may request platform mediation within 60 days of the reservation end date.

8.2 The platform's resolution team reviews evidence from both parties and makes a final binding decision within 72 hours.

8.3 Platform decisions are final. Guests may pursue external dispute resolution through their credit card company or applicable consumer protection laws.

8.4 Fraudulent claims result in account suspension and may be reported to relevant authorities.

SECTION 9: SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

9.1 GROUP BOOKINGS (10+ guests)
Group bookings follow the Strict policy regardless of the host's selected policy tier.

9.2 LAST-MINUTE BOOKINGS
Bookings made within 24 hours of check-in are subject to the host's standard policy. There is no additional grace period for last-minute bookings.

9.3 BACK-TO-BACK BOOKINGS
If a guest books consecutive stays at the same property, each booking is treated independently for cancellation purposes.

9.4 BUSINESS TRAVEL
Guests booking under a verified business account are subject to the same cancellation policies as personal bookings. No special business travel exceptions apply.
